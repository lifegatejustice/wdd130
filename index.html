Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in Africa.Nigeria is often called the "Giant of Africa." This name comes from the vastness of its land, the diversity of its peoples and languages, its huge population (the largest in Africa), and its oil and other natural resources. It is bordered by Chad and Cameroon to the east, Niger to the north and Republic of Benin in the west. It is a multinational state inhabited by more than 250 ethnic groups speaking 500 distinct languages, all identifying with a wide variety of cultures.
